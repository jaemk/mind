Meld

Zero-config discovery finds skills, agents, rules, and tools in any git repo. A mind.toml is optional, enabling advanced features.

Learn

A centralized installation-store manages symlinks across all of your agent homes. Learn everything a source has to offer or choose items piecemeal.

Sync & upgrade

Stay in sync with your melded sources, and track the installation state and availability of upgrades on an individual item level.
